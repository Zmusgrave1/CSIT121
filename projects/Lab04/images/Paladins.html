Welcome to the World of Warcraft: The First Four Paladins

This is a picture of the first four Paladins in the lore of the Warcraft universe. Their names are Uther the Lightbringer, Tirion Fordring, Saidan Dathrohan, and Turalyon. I chose this image because of the importance these four characters have on the overall story of the entire franchise as a whole.
